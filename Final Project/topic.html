Released: January 26, 2023

Developer: "Pizza Tower" is developed by a solo developer named "PizzaTowerGuy." The developer is known for their passion for retro-style platformer games and has been actively working on creating "Pizza Tower."

Released: April 26, 2023

Developer: Honkai: Star Rail is developed by Hoyoverse, a Chinese video game development company known for their popular titles such as Honkai Impact 3rd and Genshin Impact.

Utilizing the right elements against enemies can result in increased damage and additional effects.
